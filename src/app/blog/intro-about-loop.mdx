### Introducing Loop: The Who, What, and Why Behind the Project

#### **What is Loop?**

Loop is a new application designed to revolutionize how we plan trips and events. Inspired by the need for seamless collaboration and comprehensive planning, Loop brings people together in real-time to coordinate itineraries, manage budgets, and share ideas. Whether you're organizing a weekend getaway or a complex vacation itinerary, Loop ensures everyone stays in the loop.

#### **Why Build Loop?**

Planning trips is often chaotic. Between juggling dates, managing budgets, tracking reservations, and keeping everyone on the same page, it can feel overwhelming. Loop tackles this by offering a centralized platform where users can:

- Collaborate on itineraries and checklists.
- Track expenses and manage budgets.
- Share documents like tickets and bookings.
- Get recommendations for locations and activities.
- Vote on group decisions, ensuring everyone's voice is heard.

#### **The Tech Stack Behind Loop**

We’ve chosen a modern and robust tech stack to build Loop, ensuring a smooth and feature-rich user experience:

- **NextJS**: For server-side rendering and a seamless user interface.
- **Tailwind**: To keep styling efficient and maintainable.
- **TypeScript**: For safer, scalable code.
- **Mintlify**: To power our documentation and blog.
- **Sentry**: For monitoring and catching errors early.
- **Neon**: A scalable Postgres database service.
- **Kinde**: For simple authentication and user management.
- **Prisma**: For an intuitive database ORM.
- **Motion**: To create delightful animations.
- **Accessibility Tools**: To ensure the app is inclusive and user-friendly.

#### **Our MVP Goals**

To focus on delivering the core value of Loop, we’re prioritizing the following features for the Minimum Viable Product (MVP):

- **Printing and Mobile-Friendly Views**: Users can easily print or access a mobile-optimized version of their plans.
- **Undo and Redo Actions**: Simple version control for edits.
- **Trip Information Section**: Includes dates, attendees, and a customizable location image.
- **Location-Based Recommendations**: Tailored suggestions for activities and stops.
- **Reservations and Notes**: A dedicated space for bookings, attachments, and comments.
- **Budget Management**: A place to track expenses and allocate funds.
- **Collaborative Lists and Checklists**: Work together on packing lists, to-dos, and more.
- **Places to Visit/Stops**: Displaying images and details of locations.
- **Itinerary Builder**: Organized by date, with subheadings for places, times, and costs.
- **Real-Time Updates and Notifications**: Keeping everyone informed instantly.
- **Group Polling**: Voting on locations, accommodations, and activities.
- **Map Integration**: A visual representation of all planned locations.

#### **Why We’re Excited**

Loop isn’t just another planning tool; it’s a way to bring people together, streamline decision-making, and reduce the stress of organizing. From small getaways to grand vacations, Loop makes planning accessible, efficient, and enjoyable.
